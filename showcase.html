About Me

The Photographer

I believe the best moments are unposed — the laughter, the tears, and the quiet smiles in between.

With over 8 years of experience capturing love stories across India, I specialize in creating timeless wedding photography that tells your unique story. My approach blends candid moments with artistic compositions, ensuring every emotion is preserved forever.

Every wedding is a once-in-a-lifetime event, and I treat it as such. From the nervous excitement of the morning preparations to the joyful celebrations of the reception, I'm there to capture it all with an unobtrusive yet attentive eye.
